Genshin Impact game

Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by HoYoverse. It was released for Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, iOS, and Android in 2020, and on PlayStation 5 in 2021

Meet the archons!
The anemo archon Barbatos also known as Venti. He rules over mondstadt.
The geo archon Morax also known as Zhongli. He rules over Liyue.
The electro archon Belzebub also known as Raiden Ei. She is the shogun of Inazuma.
The dendro archon, nothing is known about this mysterious child.

Genshin Impact is an amazing open-world adventure that draws heavily on both its Breath of the Wild and anime inspirations to create something truly special. Even if the gacha model introduces some undesirable level grinding deeper in, the excellent combat, addictive exploration, and beautiful world make this one of the most exciting games I've played all year.
